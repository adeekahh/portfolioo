Intermedia Solutions

Timeframe

1 month

Team Members

Deliverable

Prototype, Website

Context

Freelance Project

Intermedia Solutions is a video production company based in Copenhagen operated by university students.

The company's main activity is recording speeches at events and conferences but they have also worked with commercials, branding and animation before.

Their current goal is to reach a wider customer base and through that to get clients from outside Denmark. To achieve that they needed to have a website that they can confidently advertise their services through and that can convert leads.

Process

The process of making the website started with a workshop where I gained insights from their current and aspired customers and got and understanding of the reasons behind their achievements and struggles as a company. Based on the insights we constructed 3 user personas that later helped us in determining the potential user flows and goals on the website.

Personas

Already a client
→needs to find contact information easily, have to feel that they can recommend the services to their network
→Actively looking for a video production company  trustworthiness, professionalism - goals : find a company that can be trusted with regards to timeframe, quality and value for their investment - struggles: turns out halfway through the process that they made the wrong decision and might be responsible for reporting to their executives
→ Not actively looking for a video production company  might be looking in the future so have to make an impression and make a good impression for long-term opportunities

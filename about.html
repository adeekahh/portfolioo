About me

Welcome to my website

My name is Ádám Molnár and I am a digital designer mostly working on frontend design and UX design.

My work is focused around building visually pleasing, digital products that serve as a solution to the given problem.

I am currently the in-house designer for Intermedia Video Solutions and front-end designer at ProjektX, a danish creative agency that offers design services executed by university students.

If you like my work and would like to collaborate or hire me, please contact me, I love hearing about new ideas.
